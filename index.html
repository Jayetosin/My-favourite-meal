RECIPE FOR POUNDED YAM AND EGUSI(MELON) SOUP

My Favourite food is pounded yam with Egusi(Melon) soup

Origin of the food

Pounded yam is a Nigerian food native to some ethnic groups such as the Yorubas, Tiv and Ebira, the Yorubas in Ondo State,Edo State,Ekiti State and the Ijesha people of Osun State likes the food a lot it is their cultural food.

Egusi soup which can be also called melon soup can be prepare with vegetables andother varities like meat, fish, ponmo, periwinkles etc.

Pounded yam

 Egusi Soup
Tuber Yam Melon seeds(2 cups)
Morter Palm oil(1 cup)
Pestle Vegetable
A clean pot Fresh pepper/ Chili pepper
Knife Dried/Fresh fish
Meat
Ponmo
Seasoning(Salt and Maggi[2 cubes])
Onions (1 medium size)

Steps to prepare pounded Yam

Step 1

Use a sharp knife to peel the yam. Get rid of any discolorations or spots.

Step 2

Pour into the water and wash off all dirt immediately after cutting into large chunks.

Step 3

Using a medium pot, cook the yams under high heat with enough water to cover them. Taste and adjust salt if necessary.

Step 4

Boil for approximately 20 minutes until tender. Drain yams thoroughly in a colander immediately after removing them from the heat.

Step 5

Keep your mortar and pestle clean by washing them. Pounded yam until smooth and stretchy in a mortar.

Step 6

When necessary, add water while pounding.

Step 7

In a bowl, serve the soup immediately after rolling the balls and wrapping them in plastic.

Steps to preparing Egusi (Melon) Soup

  1. In a medium pot, place Meat,Ponmo and the dried fish in the pot after it has been rinsed.
  2. Add 1 onion, 2 Maggi cubes, and salt to taste.
  3. Boil the Meat, Ponmo and dried fish for about 30-35 minutes
  4. Using a blender, grind the Melon seeds till it smooth
  5. Grind the fresh peppers and onions.Place onion pepper mixture in a bowl and set aside.
  6. Slice andRinse the vegatables until its without sand
  7. The meat, Ponmo and Dried fish should be tender when finished cooking. Remove from heat and pour it in a clean bowl.
  8. Pour Palm oil into a clean pot and allow it to get hot
  9. Add the blended pepper and onions to fry it
  10. Mix the blended Melon seeds into a paste and add it to the blended pepper and Onions
  11. Add the Seasoning to taste, also add the meat,ponmo add dried fish and a little water, Allow it to boil for about 2 minutes
  12. Add the vegetable and stir together and allow it to cook for 2 minutes

The food is Ready, Enjoy your meal
